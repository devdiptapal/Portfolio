Core Principles

My Approach to Data & Product

  • Ask if something is important, over interesting
  • Share Stories, not data points or analyses
  • Most of social media / consumer tech is sensitivity > specificity
  • Build supply, then demand. The latter is more elastic
  • People are multi-faceted and titles are limiting
  • Have opinions but don't be married to them
  • Fix a leaky bucket before turning on the faucet
  • Instead of making the perfect claypot the first day, make one claypot everyday
  • 99.9999% decisions are reversible. Take all the shots in the interest of learning
  • Marginal utility of your time to you > marginal utility of your time to a company

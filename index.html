Core Principles

My Approach to Data & Product

  • Ask if something is important, over interesting
  • Share stories, not just data points or analyses
  • In consumer tech, sensitivity often outweighs specificity
  • Build supply first, then demand - the latter is more elastic
  • Have opinions but don't be married to them
  • Fix a leaky bucket before turning on the faucet
  • Iterate daily rather than perfecting once - consistent progress beats perfection
  • Most decisions are reversible - prioritize learning over perfect planning

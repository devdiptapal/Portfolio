Future Vision

Where I'm going..

Guiding principles that continue despite milestones and shape my approach to data, product, and life.

Vulnerability

Believe that we're all somehow connected and have the larger perspective on things. Embrace openness and authenticity in all interactions.

Honesty

Invest in the truth for the good and the bad. Build trust through transparency and genuine communication.

Redemption

Try as hard as you can, sometimes you fall. This is not permanent. Get up and seize the next day with renewed energy.

Perseverance

Stick it out for things that matter. You define the things that matter. Stay committed to your core values and goals.

Intellectual Vitality

The ability to tackle seemingly uncomfortable and abstract topics under the sun with an open mind and reasoning from first principles.

Grit

Combines with perseverance with a single goal in mind. Maintain focus and determination through challenges.

Drive

An innate, biologically determined urge to attain a goal or satisfy a need. Channel this energy into meaningful pursuits.

Question Status Quo

Question the "status quo" that is usually accepted. Reason for relevance and challenge conventional thinking.

Openness

Be open to changing your mind, often. It's powerful. Embrace new perspectives and continuous learning.

Candor

Powerful to allow for new ideas to emerge. People reciprocate and mimic candor. Foster honest dialogue.

Humor

With the perspective that we're on a ball of dust floating through space, you'd only be left with the humor of it. Maintain perspective and joy.

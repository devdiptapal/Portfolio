Vision & Values

Guiding principles that shape my approach to data, product, and life.

I embrace openness and authenticity, believing that genuine connection and vulnerability foster stronger teams and better outcomes.

Honesty

I prioritize transparency and honest communication, building trust and credibility in every collaboration.

Redemption

I view setbacks as opportunities for growth, always striving to learn, adapt, and return stronger.

Perseverance

I remain committed to my goals and values, persevering through challenges to achieve meaningful results.

I approach complex problems with curiosity and an open mind, leveraging first-principles thinking to drive innovation.

Grit

I maintain focus and determination, consistently pushing through obstacles to achieve ambitious objectives.

Drive

I channel my intrinsic motivation into purposeful work, always seeking to make a positive impact.

Question Status Quo

I challenge conventional thinking, always questioning the status quo to find better solutions.

Openness

I welcome new perspectives and continuous learning, adapting readily to change.

Candor

I foster honest dialogue, encouraging candor to spark innovation and collaboration.

Humor

I maintain perspective and bring humor to my work, believing that joy and levity fuel creativity and resilience.

Where I'm going..

Values

Guiding principles that continue despite milestones.

Vulnerability

Believe that we're all somehow connected and have the larger perspective on things, you will slowly realize that being more vulnerable is an organic process.

Redemption

Try as hard as you can, sometimes you fall. This is not permanent. Getting up and running again is redemption.

Anti-establishment

Question the “default” or "status quo" that is usually accepted by society. Pure gold lies therein.

Honesty

Invest in the truth and not lie to yourself. Be honest for the good and the bad for things that matter.

Perseverance

Stick it out for things that matter. You define the things that matter.

Intellectual vitality

The ability to seemingly uncomfortable and abstract topics under the sun with an open mind and reasoning from first principles.

Grit

Combines with perseverance with a single goal in mind.

Drive

An innate, biologically determined urge to attain a goal or satisfy a need.

Flux

Move when too comfortable. A period of uncertainty serves as the training ground for growth and clarity emerges

Openness

Be open to changing your mind, often. It's powerful

Candor

Powerful to allow for new ideas to emerge. People reciprocate and mimic candor.

Humor

With the perspective that we're on a ball of dust floating through space, you'd only be left with the humor of it
